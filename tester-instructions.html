KEP v6.2 • First Test

Simple instructions for trusted testers.

Test KEP slowly. Try to break it. Tell us what feels confusing, wrong, slow, or helpful.

Tester mission

You are not judging the final product. You are helping shape it before students depend on it.

Step 1

Student flow

  1. Open Student Home.
  2. Open Practice.
  3. Answer at least 5 questions.
  4. Check result saves.
  5. Open Progress Analytics.
Step 2

Mock flow

  1. Open Mock Exam.
  2. Start the starter mock.
  3. Finish the mock.
  4. Check score and progress.
  5. Report any confusing question.
Step 3

Content feedback

  • Is the question clear?
  • Is the answer correct?
  • Is the explanation helpful?
  • Is the wording student-friendly?
Step 4

Submit feedback

Use the feedback page for design, bugs, and ideas. Use the question report page for wrong or unclear questions.
